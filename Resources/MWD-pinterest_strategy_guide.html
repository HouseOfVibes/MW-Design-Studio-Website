Pinterest is a visual discovery engine that inspires users to find ideas for their projects and interests. Unlike other social media platforms focused on social connection, Pinterest is fundamentally a search and discovery tool where users actively seek solutions, products, and inspiration.

Why Pinterest is Perfect for Business

Pinterest users have high purchase intent, spend 2x more per month than users on other platforms, and 83% of weekly Pinners have made purchases based on Pins they discovered. It's where discovery turns into action.

Pinterest vs. Other Platforms

  • Users come to Pinterest to plan and discover, not just socialize
  • Content has a longer lifespan - Pins can drive traffic for months or years
  • 97% of searches are unbranded, giving all businesses equal opportunity
  • Platform prioritizes helpful, actionable content over follower count
  • Higher income demographics make it ideal for premium products

Pinterest Account Setup & Optimization

Business Account Setup

  • Convert to Pinterest Business account for analytics and features
  • Verify your website to gain access to Rich Pins
  • Complete business profile with location and contact information
  • Set up Pinterest Shopping if you sell products
  • Install Pinterest tag for conversion tracking
  • Connect other social media accounts

Profile Optimization

Element Best Practice SEO Impact
Profile Name Include primary keyword (max 30 characters) High - appears in search results
Username Brand name or main keyword Medium - part of profile URL
Bio Description 160 characters with keywords and value proposition High - searchable content
Profile Photo High-quality logo (165x165px) Low - brand recognition
Website URL Link to homepage or landing page High - drives traffic

Board Strategy

  • Create 10-15 boards initially, focusing on your main topics
  • Use keyword-rich board titles (2-3 words work best)
  • Write detailed board descriptions with relevant keywords
  • Organize boards logically - most important boards first
  • Use board covers that match your brand aesthetic
  • Create seasonal boards to capture trending searches
  • Include a mix of your content and curated content
Rich Pins Setup

Rich Pins automatically sync information from your website to your Pins. Types include:
Product Pins: Real-time pricing and availability
Recipe Pins: Ingredients, cook time, and serving info
Article Pins: Headlines, author, and story description
App Pins: Install button for mobile apps

Creating Winning Pins

Pin Design Specifications

Square Pin 1000 x 1000px 1:1 Under 20MB

Pin Design Best Practices

  • Use bright, high-contrast colors that stand out in feeds
  • Include text overlay with 20 characters or less
  • Maintain consistent brand colors and fonts
  • Show the product or result prominently
  • Use lifestyle images rather than stock photos
  • Include your logo subtly (small, in corner)
  • Test different image styles for the same content
  • Use vertical orientation for maximum visibility

Pin Copy Optimization

Pin Title Formula

Primary Keyword + Benefit + Emotional Hook
Example: "Easy 10-Minute Breakfast Recipes That Will Make Your Mornings Better"
Keep titles under 100 characters for full display

  • Start with your primary keyword in the title
  • Write descriptions between 100-200 characters
  • Include 3-5 relevant hashtags naturally in description
  • Add a clear call-to-action (Save, Click, Try)
  • Include seasonal keywords when relevant
  • Use Pinterest's guided search suggestions for keyword ideas

Video Pin Strategy

  • Keep videos under 15 seconds for maximum impact
  • Start with an attention-grabbing first frame
  • Include captions since many users watch without sound
  • Show step-by-step processes or transformations
  • Use trending audio when relevant
  • Create square videos for better mobile viewing

Pinterest Shopping Strategy

Shopping Features Overview

  • Product Rich Pins show real-time pricing and availability
  • Shopping ads appear natively in users' feeds and search results
  • Product catalogs auto-create pins from your inventory
  • Try-on features available for beauty and fashion products
  • Shopping spotlights showcase featured products
  • Verified merchant program builds trust with shoppers

Setting Up Pinterest Shopping

  • Convert to Pinterest Business account
  • Verify your website domain
  • Install Pinterest tag on your website
  • Upload your product catalog (CSV or data feed)
  • Apply for verified merchant status
  • Enable shopping features in your account settings
  • Create product-focused boards and pins

Pinterest Ad Policies

Pinterest has strict policies on promoted content. Avoid weight loss claims, get-rich-quick schemes, and misleading before/after images. Focus on helpful, authentic content that provides genuine value to users.

Advanced Pinterest Strategies

Fresh Pin Strategy

Pinterest rewards fresh content with higher distribution. Create multiple pins for your best-performing content with different designs, titles, and descriptions.

  • Create 3-5 different pin designs for popular blog posts
  • Space out fresh pins by 2-3 months
  • Test different styles: text overlay vs. lifestyle photos
  • Vary pin titles and descriptions for keyword diversity
  • Monitor which versions perform best
  • Pin to different relevant boards

Story Pins Mastery

  • Create multi-page stories that tell complete narratives
  • Include interactive elements like lists and text overlays
  • Use Story Pins for tutorials and step-by-step guides
  • Add music and voiceovers when relevant
  • Include shopping tags for product-related stories
  • Optimize each page for mobile viewing
  • End with a clear call-to-action

Common Pinterest Mistakes to Avoid

Content & Strategy Mistakes

  • Creating horizontal images instead of vertical pins
  • Over-watermarking images or using intrusive branding
  • Treating Pinterest like other social media platforms
  • Focusing on follower count instead of engagement and traffic
  • Not optimizing pin descriptions for SEO
  • Pinning only your own content without curating others'
  • Ignoring seasonal trends and planning
  • Not creating multiple pins for the same content

Technical Mistakes

  • Not verifying your website for Rich Pins
  • Missing Pinterest tag installation for tracking
  • Poor image quality or low-resolution pins
  • Broken links or pins leading to 404 pages
  • Not organizing boards strategically
  • Inconsistent pinning schedule
  • Neglecting Pinterest Analytics insights

SEO & Discovery Mistakes

  • Using vague or non-descriptive pin titles
  • Keyword stuffing in descriptions
  • Not researching Pinterest-specific keywords
  • Ignoring trending hashtags and seasonal keywords
  • Not updating board descriptions with relevant keywords
  • Missing opportunities in Pinterest suggested searches
Pinterest Algorithm Factors

Pinterest's algorithm considers domain quality, pinner quality, pin quality, and topic relevance. Focus on creating high-quality content that users find helpful rather than trying to game the system.
