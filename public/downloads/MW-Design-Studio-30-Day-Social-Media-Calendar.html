How to Use This Template

This comprehensive social media planning template will help you organize, plan, and track your content for 30 days. Follow these steps to maximize your social media success.

Step 1: Plan Your Content

Review the Content Category Guide and plan your posts using the 5 content types. Aim for a balanced mix throughout the month.

Step 2: Fill Daily Boxes

For each day, fill in the theme, select platforms, write your post idea, add relevant hashtags, and note the best posting time.

Step 3: Use Hashtag Guide

Reference the hashtag research section to find relevant tags for your industry. Mix popular and niche hashtags for best reach.

Step 4: Track Performance

Use the engagement tracking sheet to monitor your post performance and identify what content works best for your audience.

Content Category Guide

Educational Content
Share valuable tips, tutorials, how-to content, and industry insights that help your audience learn and grow.
Examples: Industry tips, step-by-step tutorials, myth-busting facts, expert interviews, infographics with data
2-3 times per week
Behind-the-Scenes
Give your audience a peek into your company culture, team spotlights, and process reveals.
Examples: Team introductions, office tours, work-in-progress shots, day-in-the-life content, company events
1-2 times per week
Promotional Content
Highlight your products, services, special offers, and important business announcements.
Examples: Product features, sales announcements, new launches, special offers, client success stories
1-2 times per week
Community Engagement
Engage with your audience through user-generated content, testimonials, Q&A sessions, and community highlights.
Examples: Customer spotlights, user-generated content, Q&A sessions, polls, community challenges
2-3 times per week

Hashtag Research Guide

Mix high-reach, medium-reach, and niche hashtags for optimal discovery. Use 5-10 relevant hashtags per post for best results.

General Business

#SmallBusiness #Entrepreneur #BusinessOwner #LocalBusiness #SmallBiz #BusinessTips #StartupLife #BusinessSuccess #Leadership

Industry-Specific

Research hashtags specific to your industry, location, and niche. Include your city, services, and professional expertise areas.

Platform Optimization

Instagram: 5-10 hashtags, LinkedIn: 3-5 hashtags, Facebook: 1-3 hashtags, TikTok: 3-5 trending hashtags

Research Tools

Use Hashtagify, RiteTag, or native platform search to find trending and relevant hashtags in your industry.

Pro Tips for Social Media Success

Content Creation

  • Batch create content to save time
  • Use consistent visual branding
  • Focus on quality over quantity
  • Repurpose content across platforms
  • Plan seasonal content in advance

Posting Strategy

  • Post when your audience is most active
  • Use scheduling tools for consistency
  • Engage with comments within 1 hour
  • Share user-generated content
  • Cross-promote between platforms

Engagement Tactics

  • Ask questions to encourage comments
  • Use polls and interactive features
  • Respond to all comments personally
  • Share behind-the-scenes content
  • Collaborate with other businesses

Performance Tracking

  • Monitor engagement rates weekly
  • Track your best-performing content
  • Adjust strategy based on analytics
  • Set monthly growth goals
  • Use insights to improve content
