About This Brand Audit

This comprehensive brand audit checklist will help you evaluate your current brand's effectiveness across four critical areas: visual identity, messaging consistency, digital presence, and competitive positioning.

Use this tool to identify strengths and weaknesses in your brand, then develop a strategic plan to improve your market position and customer perception.

How to Use This Checklist

  • Work through each section systematically
  • Check the box for each item that your brand currently meets
  • Be honest in your assessment - this is for your benefit
  • Use the scoring guide to evaluate your overall brand health
  • Focus on unchecked items as priority improvement areas
