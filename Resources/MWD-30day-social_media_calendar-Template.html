30-Day Social Media Content Calendar

Professional Template for Small Businesses

How to Use This Template

This comprehensive social media planning template will help you organize, plan, and track your content for 30 days. Follow these steps to maximize your social media success.

Step 1: Plan Your Content

Review the Content Category Guide and plan your posts using the 5 content types. Aim for a balanced mix throughout the month.

Step 2: Fill Daily Boxes

For each day, fill in the theme, select platforms, write your post idea, add relevant hashtags, and note the best posting time.

Step 3: Use Hashtag Guide

Reference the hashtag research section to find relevant tags for your industry. Mix popular and niche hashtags for best reach.

Step 4: Track Performance

Use the engagement tracking sheet to monitor your post performance and identify what content works best for your audience.

Step 5: Check Off Completed

Mark the checkbox when you've posted content for each day. This helps you stay consistent with your posting schedule.

Step 6: Review & Improve

At the end of 30 days, review your tracking data and tips section to improve your strategy for the next month.

Quick Reference

Content Types:
Educational, Behind-the-Scenes, Promotional, Community, Entertainment
Hashtag Count:
Use 5-10 relevant hashtags per post
Posting Frequency:
1-2 posts per day across platforms

Content Category Guide

Educational Content
Share valuable tips, tutorials, how-to content, and industry insights that help your audience learn and grow. Position your brand as a trusted authority in your field.
Examples: Industry tips, step-by-step tutorials, myth-busting facts, expert interviews, infographics with data, "Did you know?" posts
2-3 times per week
Behind-the-Scenes
Give your audience a peek into your company culture, team spotlights, and process reveals. Build authentic connections by showing the human side of your business.
Examples: Team introductions, office tours, work-in-progress shots, day-in-the-life content, company events, workspace setups
1-2 times per week
Promotional Content
Highlight your products, services, special offers, and important business announcements. Keep this balanced with value-driven content to avoid appearing too sales-focused.
Examples: Product features, sales announcements, new launches, special offers, client success stories, testimonials
1-2 times per week
Community Engagement
Engage with your audience through user-generated content, testimonials, Q&A sessions, and community highlights. Foster a sense of belonging and connection.
Examples: Customer spotlights, user-generated content, Q&A sessions, polls, community challenges, reviews and responses
2-3 times per week
Entertainment Content
Share fun facts, trending topics, holiday content, and lighter content that entertains your audience. Keep your brand relatable and engaging.
Examples: Fun facts, relevant memes, holiday posts, trending challenges, industry humor, motivational quotes
1-2 times per week

Hashtag Research Guide

Mix high-reach, medium-reach, and niche hashtags for optimal discovery. Research hashtag volume and engagement before using. Use 5-10 relevant hashtags per post for best results.

General Business

#SmallBusiness #Entrepreneur #BusinessOwner #LocalBusiness #SmallBiz #BusinessTips #StartupLife #BusinessSuccess #Leadership

Industry-Specific

Research hashtags specific to your industry, location, and niche. Include your city, services, and professional expertise areas.

Engagement Hashtags

#MondayMotivation #ThrowbackThursday #FeatureFriday #TipTuesday #WisdomWednesday #ThankfulThursday #FridayFeeling

Content-Specific

#BehindTheScenes #CustomerStory #TeamSpotlight #DidYouKnow #HowTo #Tutorial #AskTheExpert #ClientSuccess

Research Tips

Use tools like Hashtagify, RiteTag, or native platform search to find trending and relevant hashtags in your industry.
